The bigger brother of the Kit Kat®

Chocolatey, Crunchy, and Creamy

The Kit Kat® CHUNKY is the larger single bar version of the classic Kit Kat® Bar. Much like the original, the CHUNKY consists of crispy wafer and milk chocolate. Unlike the original, the Kit Kat® CHUNKY has four wafers, each surrounded by delicious milk chocolate, all of which is surrounded by a thick layer of chocolate.

The Kit Kat® CHUNKY was introduced in 1999 in the UK and was an instant hit with consumers. It was then released internationally with the same praise.

When you want to take a bigger break!

Sugar, Modified milk ingredients, Wheat flour, Cocoa mass, Cocoa butter, Palm, Palm kernel, Coconut and vegetable oils, Sunflower and soy lecithin, Yeast, Sodium bicarbonate, Salt, Natural flavours

The original four-finger bar was developed after a worker at Rowntree's York Factory put a suggestion in a recommendation box for a snack that "a man could take to work in his pack".
